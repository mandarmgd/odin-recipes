Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a Chinese dish made with cooked rice that is then stir-fried in a wok with other ingredients, like meat, seafood, vegetables, and eggs, and then seasoned with a savory sauce.

It is a fast, easy, delicious, and budget-friendly meal that you can have with ingredients on hand at any time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil, divided
  • 3 cups cooked and cooled rice
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
